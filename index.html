Privacy Policy — Go VPN

Free, no-logs VPN for Android · by H93

Effective date: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Go VPN (package com.h93lab.govpn), provided by H93 (“we”, “us”, “our”), handles information when you use our Android application. We built Go VPN to be private by design: we do not log, monitor, or store your VPN traffic. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

Our No-Logs Promise

We do not log, monitor, inspect, record, or store any of your VPN traffic — including the websites you visit, the apps you use, your DNS queries, or the content of any data sent through the tunnel. Your online activity stays yours.

Information We Collect

Go VPN has no user accounts and requires no sign-up. We collect only the minimum needed to provision a VPN connection:

  • Anonymous device identifier — a randomly generated UUID created on your device on first launch. It is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any personal identity, and is used solely to issue and re-use your VPN configuration.
  • Connection timestamps — the date and time a device requests a connection, used only to operate and maintain the service.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We deliberately do not collect, log, or store any of the following:

  • Browsing history — the sites or services you access.
  • DNS queries — the domains your device looks up.
  • Traffic content — any data transmitted through the VPN tunnel.
  • IP addresses — we do not record your originating or assigned IP address.
  • Personal information — no name, email, phone number, location, or contacts.

Third-Party Services

Go VPN relies on a small number of third-party providers. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

Google AdMob

Serves the in-app advertisements that keep Go VPN free. AdMob may use device-level advertising identifiers to deliver and measure ads. See the Google Privacy Policy.

Supabase

Stores the anonymous device configuration (the UUID and VPN config) that lets your device reconnect. No personal data is stored. See the Supabase Privacy Policy.

VPN Traffic

When connected, your traffic is encrypted and routed through our VPN server. We act purely as a conduit: we do not log, monitor, or store any user traffic passing through the tunnel. We cannot and do not associate any browsing activity with your device.

Data Retention

The anonymous device identifier and connection timestamps are retained only for as long as needed to operate the service. You can clear all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its data in Android settings.

Children's Privacy

Go VPN is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. As we collect no personal information at all, the app does not build any profile of any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data? Reach us at support@h93lab.com.
